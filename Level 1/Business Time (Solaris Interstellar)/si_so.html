Sky Whale-class Cargo

The Sky Whale-class cargo ship is used primarily for shorter shipping distances, up to 15 parsecs

Leviathan-class Cargo

The Leviathan-class cargo ship is used primarily for longer shipping distances, greater than 15 parsecs

Hopper-class Transport

The Hopper-class cargo ship is used primarily for shorter shipping distances, up to 15 parsecs, and seats up to 100 people

Kangaroo-class Transport

The Kangaroo-class cargo ship is used primarily for shorter shipping distances, up to 15 parsecs, and seats up to 800 people

Swarm-class Fighter Squadron

The Swarm-class fighter squadron is an extra fee, only for customers who want some security for their shipment

Hive-class Fighter Squadron

The Hive-class fighter squadron is an extra fee, only for custoemrs who want some security for their shipment. The Hive is larger than the Swarm.
